About Us

Pokichat is here to make chatting with friends more private and temporary. With our app, you can create private groups where you share messages and images that disappear once the last person leaves.

We believe in giving you a space where your conversations are safe and only for you and your friends. No need to worry about your messages being stored forever.

Our goal is to keep things simple and secure, making sure your chats are easy to use and private. Thanks for choosing Pokichat. We're excited to help you connect in a fresh, new way!

How it Works

Create Your Group:

Start by setting up a private group for you and your friends. Choose who gets to join and customize your chat space.

Chat and Share:

Enjoy real-time conversations, share messages, and upload images with your group members. Everything is private and secure.

Group Disappears:

When the last person leaves the group, it automatically disappears, along with all the messages and images shared.

Start Fresh:

Create new groups anytime for fresh conversations and experiences. Your privacy is always our priority!
